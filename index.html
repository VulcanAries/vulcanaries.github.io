Fire & Steel: A Fallout 76 Tale

Fire & Steel is an audio drama set in the world of Fallout 76, reimagining and expanding the Brotherhood of Steel storyline in Appalachia.

The story begins in the aftermath of the Brotherhood’s arrival at Fort Atlas, when tensions within the First Expeditionary Force threaten to fracture their mission. As Knight Daniel Shin, Paladin Rahmani, and the soldiers under their command struggle to bring order to the wasteland, a Vault Dweller named Ophelia becomes entangled in their fight—carrying secrets that could change everything.

What begins as a mission to restore stability to Appalachia soon reveals deeper conflicts: political divisions within the Brotherhood, hidden alliances, and enemies working from the shadows. As trust is tested and the cost of duty grows heavier, the people of Fort Atlas must decide what the Brotherhood truly stands for—and what they are willing to sacrifice to protect the future.

Blending character-driven storytelling, action, and emotional drama, Fire & Steel explores loyalty, responsibility, and the fragile hope of rebuilding a world after its end.
